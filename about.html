About Us

BlackPink is a Korean Girl Group...

Blackpink is a South Korean girl group that was formed by YG Entertainment. The group debuted on August 8, 2016, and quickly gained international recognition for their music and performances. Blackpink is known for their unique blend of K-pop, pop, hip-hop, and EDM influences, and they have become one of the most popular and successful K-pop groups in the world.
